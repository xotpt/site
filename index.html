about

I write. I read. I translate. A lover of language, I learned Chinese through rigorous study and Spanish through pure immersion. Now I'm focused on my fiction.

Born in rural Maine, raised in rural Illinois, and educated in rural Vermont, I eventually found my way to a city. I lived in Shanghai for a time before moving to Buenos Aires, where I currently reside.

PROJECTS

Cherry Cherry

Cherry Cherry is a novella about a young girl, Sophie, and her obsession with her best friend’s older sister in the summer before high school. Sophie is pained by possibility and the fleeting, aching beauty of being an adolescent girl. Here, she realizes that even the most momentous events fade with time.
